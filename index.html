Who this is for

You're a Business School Student, consultant, product manager, founder, or analyst. You don't think of yourself as technical. You've used ChatGPT and you've seen colleagues do impressive things with AI tools, but you're not sure how to get from "interesting demos" to "I built something my team actually uses."

This course closes that gap. By the end you'll have shipped at least one working tool — and you'll know when to use these techniques and, just as importantly, when not to.

What you won't find here

This is not a software engineering course. We don't teach data structures, design patterns, or how to pass a coding interview. We teach how to direct an AI agent that already knows those things, so you can focus on the business problem.
