Module 4 · 90 minutes

The vibecoding workflow

The repeatable loop that separates people who get real work done from people who fight their AI tools all day.

What you'll be able to do

Take a fresh project from "blank folder" to "working artifact" using a consistent workflow you can apply to any task: plan, set up your information environment, execute, verify, iterate.

Planned sub-pages

  • Plan before execute. Why telling the agent to plan first (and reviewing the plan) saves more time than it costs.
  • Setting up your information environment. Folder structure, what belongs in a CLAUDE.md file, and why this matters more than any prompt trick.
  • Context engineering. What to put in front of the agent, what to leave out, and how to manage long-running sessions.
  • Version safety with git. Four commands. That's it. No branching, no merging, just save-points and rollback.
  • Iterating when it goes sideways. When to nudge, when to restart, when to step back and re-plan.
