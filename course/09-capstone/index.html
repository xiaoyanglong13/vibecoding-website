Module 9 · 3+ hours

Capstone

Ship something real to someone who'll actually use it.

The brief

Choose a problem you actually have — at school, at work, or in a side project — and build a working tool that addresses it. "Working" means a real person who isn't you can use it and benefit from it. The project should take you 3–8 hours total, including writing it up.

Planned content

  • Project brief. Constraints, deliverables, what counts as "shipped".
  • Rubric. What you'll be evaluated on: problem framing, solution fit, verification rigor, write-up.
  • Peer review. Structured exchange with one other student before final submission.
  • Past projects. A gallery of capstones from previous cohorts (once we have them).
