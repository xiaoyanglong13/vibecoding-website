Module 6 · 45 minutes

Limits, risks, and responsibility

Short but non-negotiable. The judgment side of the skill.

What you'll be able to do

Handle confidential data without accidentally leaking it, understand what your usage actually costs, navigate academic integrity and professional attribution norms, and recognize when vibecoding is the wrong tool for the job.

Planned sub-pages

  • Data security. What you can and can't paste into a chatbot. Self-hosted options. Redaction patterns.
  • Cost awareness. What an API bill looks like, what drives cost, how to keep it predictable.
  • Academic integrity and attribution. Norms for citing AI assistance in coursework and at work.
  • When not to vibecode. A short, honest list of situations where you should stop and bring in someone else.
